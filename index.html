Welcome to the official digital hub. More than a landing page, this platform is an evolving media property designed to house personal memory, cultural history, and the unfinished spark of creative ambition shared between Jonathan Stathakis and Jimi Hendrix.

The Book

Jimi and Me is the cornerstone of the platform. The site will serve as the premier commercial and editorial destination for the memoir, guiding readers into the 18-month friendship and creative collaboration at the heart of the story.

The platform will showcase purchasing options, exclusive chapter excerpts, early review quotes, and a detailed exploration of the cultural context surrounding Hendrix's time in Woodstock, Shokan, and New York.

The Screen Projects

The website is built to grow into a centralized engine for the upcoming feature film and documentary projects. Hendrix originally enlisted Jonathan to help write a dialogue-free, visually ambitious motion picture.

We will bring audiences behind the scenes as that unfulfilled cinematic vision finally translates to the screen. Expect exclusive project milestones, teaser trailers, casting news, and deep-dive production journals.

The Live Event

Merging fine art and rock history, the platform will spotlight an unforgettable live concert event featuring world-renowned artist Rob Prior.

Prior will perform live on stage, painting a massive, visceral portrait of Hendrix based directly on one of Jonathan's rare personal photographs. The website will handle event ticketing, live-stream access, and post-event galleries of the final masterpiece.
